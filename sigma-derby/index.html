How to Play

"Derby"

  • Deposit Coins and push bet button.

  • Pair of horses (Quinella) bet only.

    Pair must finish first & second in any order.

  • Bet 1 to 20 per horse combination.
  • Any variety of horse combination can be bet.
  • Wins over 500 coins can be paid by attendant.
  • Push collect button before leaving.
